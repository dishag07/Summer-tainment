SUMMER 2023-- JUNIOR YR

This summer, I visited the beach and had a wonderful time. The sun was shining and the clear blue sea was enchanting. I spent the day playing in the sand, swimming in the sea, and basking in the sun. I enjoyed the cool sea breeze that swept through my hair while listening to the sound of the waves crashing against the shore. I even found some seashells and watched the colorful sunset. It was such a beautiful day and I felt refreshed and happy to have experienced it. I am looking forward to returning to the beach next summer for more outdoor fun and relaxation.
